About the Developer: Al Habtoor Group

The Al Habtoor Group, established in 1970, has evolved into one of the UAE’s most respected conglomerates. With ventures in hospitality, automotive, leasing, real estate, education, and publishing, its presence is felt not only across Dubai but in global cities like London, Vienna, and Budapest. The Group’s real estate division includes iconic developments such as the Al Habtoor Tower and The Residences, Al Habtoor Grand – hallmarks of luxury living.
